Probability & Statistics

AI Kaisa Decide Karta Hai â€” Uncertainty ka Science

AI kabhi 100% sure nahi hota. Woh hamesha probability ke saath sochta hai. Yeh section batata hai AI ka "confidence system" kaise kaam karta hai.

ًں”¹ Probability Basics

P(A) = event A ke hone ki likelihood. Range: [0, 1]. P(0) = impossible, P(1) = certain.

ًں”¹ Bayes' Theorem â€” The King of AI Reasoning

P(A|B) = P(B|A) أ— P(A) / P(B) P(A|B) = Posterior â€” B dekhne ke baad A ki probability P(B|A) = Likelihood â€” Agar A true hai toh B observe karne ki prob P(A) = Prior â€” A ke baare mein pehle se kya pata tha P(B) = Evidence â€” B ke hone ki overall probability
ًں§  Bayes AI mein kaise use hota hai

Spam filter example: "Free money" word dekha â†’ update karo: yeh email spam hone ki probability badh gayi. Nayi evidence (word) prior belief (base spam rate) ke saath combine hoti hai posterior (final decision) banane ke liye.

Modern deep learning mein Bayes explicit nahi hota â€” lekin spirit wahi hai: har training step mein model apni "beliefs" (weights) update karta hai naye data ke basis pe.
