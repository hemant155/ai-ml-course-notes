Backpropagation kya hai?
Backpropagation = backward propagation of errors.

Jab AI galat predict karta hai — woh galti backward flow karti hai through every weight. Har weight ko pata chalta hai ki woh kitna responsible tha us galti mein. Phir gradient descent se sab weights update hote hain.

Yeh sab chain rule se hota hai. "Next" dabao aur step by step dekho!
Analogy: Factory mein ek defective product nikla. Manager backward jaata hai — packing se assembly se raw material tak — aur pata lagata hai kahan galti hui. Phir har step ko thoda adjust kiya jaata hai. Yahi backprop hai.
